About

Our competitive coding group, headed by Professor James, participates in coding competitions all across the country. On our scheduled meeting times, we will work on practice problems in preparation for the next competition.

Our usual meeting room is ACE104. Each week, our scheduled meeting times will be sent out via email to every member. Please also check your email for any other updates to meeting location/method.
