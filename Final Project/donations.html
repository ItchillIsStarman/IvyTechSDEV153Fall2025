We accept donations to help fund our projects; the more funding we get, the more ambitious projects we can do!

Tier Amount Rewards (Higher tiers also get to claim rewards from the lower tiers)

Duckie $15+ Get the chance to claim a free CS-themed t-shirt from the campus store!
Lion $50+ Get featured in the credits of all the virtual projects we make (if you include your name), with the option of including a little message!
